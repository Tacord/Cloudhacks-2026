What is a Hackathon?

A hackathon is a weekend-long event where you program and build a project. You will spend the event planning, programming, testing, and presenting your creation to a panel of judges (and win prizes!)

What is CloudHacks?

CloudHacks is a student-led hackathon aiming to unite avid programmers from across the country and innovate all sorts of solutions to solve pertinent issues, global and local. It provides an opportunity for like-minded individuals to learn, innovate and network over the course of the day, through workshops and intensive programming.
